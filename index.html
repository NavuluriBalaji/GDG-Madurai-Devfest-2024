Google Devfest Madurai 2023 is our 9 edition of the devfest event where we bring sessions on the latest google technology to the developers in Madurai and the southern districts of Tamilnadu. Last year devfest we have hosted more than 10+ Google developer experts and 400+ developers. This is one of the largest tech conferences in Madurai.See More >
READ ABOUT PREVIOUS EVENTS
GDG Madurai (Google Developer Group Madurai) is a community of developers, tech enthusiasts, and professionals passionate about learning, sharing, and building with technology. It is part of the global GDG network supported by Google and hosts events like DevFest, workshops, and hackathons to explore technologies such as AI, cloud computing, mobile development, and more. Open to all skill levels, GDG Madurai provides a platform for networking, skill development, and collaboration, promoting the use of Google tools to solve real-world problems while fostering an inclusive and innovative tech community.
